Pizza Ramen

Description:

An easy and quick cyberpunk dish that's not very healthy and cheap as hell.

Ingredients:

  • Instant Ramen
  • String cheese
  • Dried tomatoes
  • Pepperoni
  • Dried oregano

Steps:

  1. Boil the ramen
  2. Drain the water
  3. Add the other ingredients (cheese, tomatoes, pepperoni and oregano)
  4. Stir
