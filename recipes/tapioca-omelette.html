Tapioca Omelette

Description:

An easy to make, fast and nutritious dish.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of tapioca flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • some slices of ham
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Add the egg, oil and salt to a recipient and mix with a fork.
  2. Add the tapioca flour and mix well.
  3. Fry the mixture both sides.
  4. Add the ham and fold the omelette.
