Mayo Pasta

Description:

A healthy (?) mixture of pasta, salad and mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • Fusilli pasta
  • Mayonnaise
  • Canned tuna
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Dried parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta as instructed in the package.
  2. Cut the cheese and tomatoes into cubes.
  3. Add the cheese, tuna and parsley to the pasta and mix slowly.
  4. Add the mayonnaise and tomatoes.
  5. Mix everything slowly and put in the fridge for a couple of hours.
